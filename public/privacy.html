Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

GrokWords is a frontend-only application. We do not run a backend or store your data on our servers. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly send content to xAI APIs for model responses, images, or voice.

What We Store Locally

  • IndexedDB: your words, generated definitions, examples, images, and grokked timestamps.
  • localStorage: your settings (e.g., API key, native language, preferences).

What We Send to xAI

  • When you generate definitions, examples, images, or use voice, the relevant word text and prompts are sent to xAI’s APIs.
  • Audio you speak in the voice modal is transmitted to xAI to generate responses.

What We Do Not Do

  • No backend server; no database on our side.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party ads.
  • No sale or sharing of personal data.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser’s storage (IndexedDB/localStorage) to remove your data.
  • You can stop using xAI features at any time; without an API key the app won’t call xAI.

Third-Party Services

Requests to xAI are subject to xAI’s own terms and privacy practices. Please review xAI documentation for details.

If you have questions, please open an issue on the project repository.
